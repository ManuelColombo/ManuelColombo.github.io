who am I?

inconvenient question, I will not be diplomatic

Today we tend to divide in small subset of skills in order to better standardization of the creative production.
I believe that the true is less simple and much more intriguing. The boundaries blurs and greatness speech with other greatness in any kind of human creation, from architecture to music, from technology to philosophy.
So who I am?
I’m a designer and I want to embrace the complexity and the contradictions of the real world not to be the sponsor of some methodology.

why?

asking the right question

My designer mission is to ask the right question or, at least, a little less wrong question over and over again.

Only asking I could reach some kind of comprehension of the needs, dreams and pains. Only with strong comprehension I could empathize with others.
True Empathy is the 90% of the designer's job, then typesetting comes easy.
