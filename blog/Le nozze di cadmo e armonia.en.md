# Le nozze di Cadmo e Armonia, by Roberto Calasso

A vast, expansive book that takes on all of Greek civilization: its myths, its divinities. It starts at the beginning, then digs even further back and deeper into myth. A recurring phrase that opens chapters, often held together by subtle threads of assonance, is "How did it all begin?"
